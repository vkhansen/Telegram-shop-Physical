## Age first

Visitors confirm they are of **legal age** before browsing the gallery. That gate is intentional.

## Lead form, not spam

We do **not** push unsolicited Telegram joins as the primary path. Adults choose:

- LINE  
- WhatsApp  
- Email  
- Phone  
- Telegram (optional, private)

## What happens after you submit

1. You receive a confirmation (when email is configured).  
2. Our team follows up on your **preferred channel**.  
3. Orders or logistics — where allowed — stay private and discreet.

## Your rights

See our [Privacy](/privacy) page. You can ask us to stop contact at any time.
