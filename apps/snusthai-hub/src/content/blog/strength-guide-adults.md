Our gallery uses a simple **1–7 strength badge** so adults can compare products at a glance. It is a **relative scale for browsing**, not a clinical dose chart.

## Lower end (about 1–3)

Often chosen by adults who prefer a lighter experience. Start lower if you are new to a format — and only if you already use nicotine products legally.

## Mid range (about 4–5)

Common everyday preference among experienced adult users.

## Higher end (6–7)

Intended for very experienced adult users only. Higher is not “better.”
